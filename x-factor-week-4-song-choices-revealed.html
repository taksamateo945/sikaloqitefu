'The X Factor' Week 4 Song Choices Revealed

April 2024 · 2 minute read

‘The X Factor’ recently unveiled that on the fourth week of Live Shows, the contestants would be performing an array of love songs – with modern and older classics, even some originals, being performed by the contestants. Judges Simon Cowell, Nicole Scherzinger, Sharon Osbourne and Louis Walsh will be able to offer their comments about each performance, but ultimately it is the public’s decision as to who stays in the competition and who leaves.

So who will be singing what on this week’s Live Show? Be sure to read on to find out!

Simon Cowell (Groups)

Rak-Su – ‘Mona Lisa’ (Original)

Sean and Conor Price – ‘Issues’ (Julia Michaels)

The Cutkelvins – ‘Saved Me From Myself’ (Original)

Nicole Scherzinger (Overs)

Kevin Davy White – ‘I Will Always Love You’ (Whitney Houston)

Matt Linnen – ‘Fallin” (Alicia Keys)

Sharon Osbourne (Girls)

Grace Davies – ‘Hesitate’ (Original)

Rai-Elle Williams – ‘Mr Big Stuff’ (Jean Knight)

Holly Tandy – ‘Love Me Harder’ (Ariana Grande and The Weeknd)

Louis Walsh (Boys)

Lloyd Macey – ‘From This Moment On’ (Shania Twain featuring Bryan White)

Sam Black – ‘Oops’ (Little Mix featuring Charlie Puth)

The show also confirmed that Fergie and 2016 ‘The X Factor’ winner Matt Terry will be performing on this weekend’s Live Shows.

‘The X Factor’ continues on ITV on Saturday 18 November at 8.10 pm and Sunday 19 November at 7.30 pm.
