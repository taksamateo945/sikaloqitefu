Hogwarts Legacy: How to Get Mongrel Fur

February 2024 · 3 minute read

Mongrel Fur is an ingredient in Hogwarts Legacy that is used for making the Edurus potion.

To get Mongrel Fur, players will need to either buy it from J. Pippins Potions or loot it after killing Dark Mongrels, which can be found in Mongrel Lairs around the map.

In this guide, I will explain how to get Mongrel Fur in Hogwarts Legacy and showcase where to use it.

If you’re interested in unlocking unique cosmetics, consider checking out our guide on how to complete the “Mer-ky Depths” sidequest, which is easy and rewards a nice outfit.

How to Get Mongrel Fur in Hogwarts Legacy

There are two ways of getting your hands on Mongrel Fur in Hogwarts Legacy. The first and easiest one is by buying it from the J. Pippins Potion store in Hogsmeade.

It comes at a price of 50 coins per fur and the store has a supply of five on every restock.

If you’re looking to craft only one or two potions just to complete Professor Sharp’s assignment, I recommend using this method.

Also, if you’re lacking money and can’t buy it, consider checking out our guide on how to make money fast in the game. As long as you can capture beasts, getting rich is fairly easy.

The second method involves looting it from enemies killed. Specifically, Dark Mongrels will always drop Mongrel Fur when killed. To find them, open the world map and zoom in once to be able to see lair locations.

Try and look for any Mongrel Lairs, which can be found in various places. For instance, there is a Mongrel Lair just east of Hogsmeade, as shown in the picture below.

With multiple Mongrels in each lair, you’re guaranteed a hefty supply of Mongrel Furs. Additionally, they are fairly easy to defeat, especially if you use some of the best spell combos in the game.

Where to Use Mongrel Fur in Howarts Legacy

Now that you’ve got enough Mongrel Fur, let’s see how to use it. This ingredient is used in making Edurus potion.

Make sure you have access to a brewing stand either in the Potions Class or in the Room of Requirement and combine Mongrel Fur with Ashwinder Eggs to create an Edurus Potion.

Ashwinder Eggs can be found all around the map, especially around rocky terrain.

The Edurus potion increases the user’s defense during combat and allows them to survive most attacks. If you’re struggling to defeat a boss fight or any combat-related quest, the Edurus potion can be helpful.

Wrapping up

In conclusion, players can get Mongrel Fur in Hogwarts Legacy by purchasing it from J. Pippins or by killing Dark Mongrel in the open world.

Then, they can combine Mongrel Fur with Ashwinder Eggs on any brewing stand to create an Edurus potion.

For more potions, consider checking out our guide on how to get more healing (Wiggenweld) potions in the game, which are essential for restoring your health.
