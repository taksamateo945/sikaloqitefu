Paul Rudd (Darby Rudd’s Father)

Paul Stephen Rudd was born on April 6, 1969, in Passaic, New Jersey, U.S.A. He is a successful American actor.

Paul is the son of Michael Rudd and Gloria Irene Granville. His parents are Ashkenazi Jewish Descendants.

He grew up in America and moved around due to his father’s occupation. He attended the University of Kansas and had a major in theater. 

Furthermore, he took acting lessons at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts and British American Drama Academy. And soon, Paul began getting offers in T.V. series.

His breakthrough came after he was cast in Clueless. The film was well-received, and soon more offers came for the actor. 

Over a couple of years, Paul has appeared in films like Anchorman: The Legend of Ron Burgundy alongside Will Ferrel, The 40-Year-Old Virgin alongside Steve Carell, Ant-man alongside Michael Douglas, etc.
