Quick facts

Jordan Peele childhood

On February 21st, 1979, Jordan was born in Manhattan, New York, to Lucinda Williams and Hayward Peele.

Jordan is a Biblical male name that means to flow down or descend.

Based on his birthdate, he is a Pisces. Pisces man is intuitive, creative, and caring; however, they can be gullible, moody, and holds grudges.

He is from a mixed-race family. His mother is white, and his father is African-American.

Lucinda single-handedly raised him as his father, and Hayward was absent from his life. Jordan’s family had to cope with hardships after his father died prematurely. However, his mother worked hard to give Jordan a happy childhood.

Peele was an introvert as a child and spent most of his time reading or drawing. However, his parents and teachers considered him to be a bright child.

Peele attended Calhoun School for his elementary education. After that, the New York native studied at Sarah Lawrence College, a private liberal arts university.

Later, he dropped out of college to form a comedy duo with his roommate, Rebecca Drysdale. The same roommate became his future “Key & Peele” co-star and writer.

This academy award winner is a famous name in American entertainment. In addition, the annual listicle, Time 100, listed Jordan as the most influential person in the world in 2017.

Jordan Peele parents

Lucinda Williams (mother)

Lucinda William has English roots. She is the daughter of Earl Haworth Williams and Josephine Helen Taylor.

Her parents came from a Colonial American background in the 1600s. They came from Maryland and Massachusetts.

After the death of her husband, the responsibility of raising their son was in Lucinda’s hands. She worked hard and did many shifts to take care of her kids. As a result, Jordan grew up seeing his mother as a role model. As a result, he often talks about her.

Lucinda Williams passed away on April 23rd, 2018, in New York City from unspecified causes.

Hayward Peele (father)

Jordan’s father, Hayward Peele, was born on April 23, 1949, in New York City, USA. His father’s name is Haywood Peele Sr, and his mother is Lucy May Doggett.

Hayward Peele was the bassist in a Jazz and Funk band. In addition, he was a full-time street musician.

Jordan’s dad, Hayward, was murdered in NY City on November 2, 2000.

Jordan Peele wife

Chelsea Peretti

Jordan Peele is married to the “Brooklyn 99” star Chelsea Peretti. She is a famous American comedian, actress, television writer, singer, and songwriter.

The wife of Jordan made a name for herself by playing Gina Linetti in Brooklyn 99, an ensemble police comedy. Her quirky personality and quirky outlook on life made her a fan favorite.

She is also a recording artist who has released a studio album called “Foam and Flotsam.” According to sources, Chelsea and Peele met through a mutual friend named Andy Samberg. Samberg also starred in “Brooklyn 99.”

In 2015 the couple got engaged and announced their marriage the following year. The couple eloped to Big Sur, California, and got married there. It was a huge surprise for everyone, even for their families.
